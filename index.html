Welcome to the virtual home of the Earth Science Society @ IIT Kanpur!

The Earth Science Society at IIT Kanpur was established in 2021 with a vision to unite the department of Earth Sciences at IIT Kanpur through various cultural, academic, and sports events.

We intend to do this by-

  • conducting events to bring faculties, staff, and students on the same platform for bonding.
  • building an ecosystem where community members will get various opportunities to develop themselves.
  • fostering a supportive and inclusive community for students and other members.

"Hey there! I’m excited to invite Earth Sciences enthusiasts to join our awesome community. With the right people and true initiatives, one can admire achieving great heights. Our team is dedicated to deliver the best in the current tenure and to bring on few initiatives which aims to favour students. Our vision is to maintain the tradition of our society by conducting cultural events and adding flavour of academia through student chapters, workshops, seminars, RSD, etc. to promote learning. We try to offer a platform for sharing knowledge along with unforgettable experiences like Teacher’s Day, Fresher’s Night, Sports Day, etc. I welcome you all to Earth Sciences Society!"

-Ayush Gupta
President

"Greetings from Earth Sciences Society! I am delighted to welcome you to our vibrant community. Here, integrity, learning, and companionship are at the heart of everything we do. From enriching academic sessions to engaging events we strive to create a nurturing environment where students can flourish and grow. My role is to make sure that the student-led-body is organizing and conducting events in a timely fashion. I encourage you to join us on this journey of exploration and collaboration, as together we uncover the wonders of Earth science. Let's embark on this adventure together!"

-Dr Indra Sekhar Sen
Faculty Advisor
